What is responsive design?

Responsive design means that the design of a webpage adjusts in its layout, which makes the content readable regardless the device. This implicates in other words that the user is able to read the content on the same webpage from a smaller device such as a mobile phone or a larger device such as a computer – without the require to scroll or zoom to achieve readability unlike traditional web design. Considering the importance of a website being mobile friendly today, responsive design is necessary for all sorts of devices in order to reach usability and satisfaction for the user.
